Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start the 10-minute timer?

The timer is pre-set to 10:00 when the page loads. Click Start, tap the ring, or press Space to begin immediately.

Does it alarm when it reaches zero?

Yes. An audio alarm plays automatically at zero. Enable browser notifications to get an alert even if the tab is minimised.

How many seconds is 10 minutes?

Exactly 600 seconds. The progress ring counts down one tick per second.

Can I set a different duration?

Yes — use the preset chips (1, 2, 5, 10, 15, 20 min) or enter any custom time and tap Set.

Will it run accurately in the background?

Yes. The countdown tracks wall-clock time, not just animation frames, so it stays accurate when you switch apps.

Is this timer free?

Yes — 100% free, no account required, works on any device in any modern browser.
