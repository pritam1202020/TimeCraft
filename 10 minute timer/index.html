FAQ — 10 Minute Timer

How do I start a 10-minute timer?The timer is already set to 10:00 when you open the page. Tap the blue Start button, tap the ring, or press Space on a keyboard. No typing needed.
Does it make a sound when done?Yes. An audio alarm plays automatically at zero. Allow browser notifications to get an alert even if this tab is minimised.
How many seconds is 10 minutes?Exactly 600 seconds. The ring depletes by one tick every second across all 600 counts.
Can I set a different duration?Yes — use the preset chips (1, 2, 5, 10, 15, 20 min) or type any custom number in the Custom field and tap Set.
Does it keep running in the background?Yes. The countdown continues accurately as long as the tab is open. The alarm fires correctly because we track wall-clock time, not just frame ticks.
What is a 10-minute timer good for?Cooking pasta or rice, focused work sprints, stretching or cool-down sessions, classroom timed exercises, short stand-up meetings, and restorative power naps.
