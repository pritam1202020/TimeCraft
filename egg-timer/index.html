How long should I boil an egg?

All times below are for large eggs (approx. 63 g) straight from the fridge, dropped into already-boiling water. For room-temperature eggs, subtract 30 seconds. For extra-large eggs, add 30–60 seconds.

🥚 Soft Boiled — 4 minutes White is just set, yolk is completely liquid and glossy. Perfect on toast soldiers, ramen, or a simple breakfast bowl.
✨ Jammy Egg — 6 minutes Custardy, flowing yolk with a golden jammy centre. The favourite for Japanese ramen eggs (ajitsuke tamago) and grain bowls.
🍳 Medium Boiled — 7 minutes A slightly soft, rich yolk that holds its shape when cut. Brilliant halved on a salad or alongside roasted vegetables.
💛 Hard Boiled — 10 minutes Fully set firm yolk, pale yellow throughout. Ideal for egg sandwiches, devilled eggs, and meal-prep protein.
🧊 Always use an ice bath Transfer eggs immediately into cold or iced water when the timer sounds. This halts carry-over cooking and makes peeling dramatically easier.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long to boil an egg for a runny yolk?

4 minutes in boiling water gives a runny, liquid yolk with a barely-set white. Use the Soft preset above to start the countdown instantly.

What is a jammy egg?

A jammy egg is boiled for 6 minutes, giving a yolk that is set on the outside but still soft and flowing in the centre — like warm jam. It is widely used in ramen and grain bowls.

Do I start timing when the water boils or when I add the eggs?

Start timing as soon as the eggs go into already-boiling water. Tap Start on this page the moment you lower the eggs in, and the countdown begins immediately.

Why are my boiled eggs hard to peel?

Very fresh eggs are harder to peel because the white adheres more firmly to the inner membrane. Using slightly older eggs and plunging into an ice bath immediately after boiling both make peeling much easier.

Does altitude affect egg boiling time?

Yes. Water boils at a lower temperature at high altitude, so eggs cook more slowly. Add 1–2 minutes for every 1,000 metres (3,300 ft) above sea level. Use the Custom input to dial in the extra time.

Will this egg timer alarm if I switch tabs?

Yes. The countdown tracks wall-clock time, so it stays accurate even if you leave the browser tab. Enable browser notifications for an alert that fires even with the tab minimised.
